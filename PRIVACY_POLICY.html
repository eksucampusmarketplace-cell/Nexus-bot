Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2025
Effective Date: January 15, 2025

📋 Overview

Thank you for using Nexus, a Telegram group management bot and Mini App (collectively, the "Service"). At Nexus, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data.

By using Nexus (our Telegram bots @NexusBot and any cloned bots, as well as our Mini App), you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

🎯 Information We Collect

1. Telegram Account Information

What we collect:
• Telegram User ID
• First name, last name, and username (if set)
• Language preference
• Profile bio (if applicable)
• Account status information

How we collect it: This information is automatically provided by Telegram's API when you start a private chat, join a group, or interact with our Mini App.

Purpose: Identify and authenticate users, enable moderation commands, provide personalized responses.

Retention: As long as your Telegram account exists, and up to 5 years after account deletion.

2. Group Membership & Activity Data

What we collect:
• Groups where you are a member
• Your role in each group (member, admin, owner, restricted, banned, left)
• Number of messages you've sent
• Date of last message and join date

Purpose: Enable group management commands, track activity for trust scores.

Retention: Up to 5 years after account deletion.

3. Administrative Permissions & Roles

⚠️ Cannot be deleted: This data is essential for the public service. To remove it, leave groups where you are an administrator.

What we collect: Groups where you're admin/owner, permissions, custom titles, bot-side roles.

Retention: As long as you maintain the role in the group.

4. Moderation Records

⚠️ Cannot be deleted: Essential for the public service.

What we collect: Warnings issued, timestamps, expiration dates, reasons.

Retention: Until warnings are removed by admins or expire.

5. Bot Clone Information

What we collect: Bot clones you've created, usernames, encrypted tokens.

Note: Deleting this data will cause your clones to stop functioning.

6. Mini App Usage & Preferences

What we collect: Language preferences, group settings, usage patterns, authentication tokens (encrypted).

Retention: Up to 5 years after account deletion.

7. Captcha & Anti-Bot Data

⚠️ Cannot be deleted: Essential for the public service.

What we collect: Groups joined but not completed captcha, expiration times, failed attempts.

Retention: Active: 30 minutes | Failed history: 2 months

8. Music Player Data

What we collect: Music queues, play history, playlists, volume preferences.

Retention: Active queue: while playing | History: 30 days | Playlists: until deleted

9. Trust Score & Behavioral Data

What we collect: Trust score (0-100), activity metrics, moderation history, engagement data.

Retention: Up to 5 years after account deletion.

10. Support & Feedback

What we collect: Support messages, dates, content, group IDs, screenshots/logs.

Retention: Up to 5 years after account deletion.

11. Stars Economy & Payments (if applicable)

What we collect: Purchase history, payment methods, transaction IDs.

Note: Deleting this data will revoke access to purchased features.

12. Channel Management Data

What we collect: Linked channels, scheduled posts, post history.

Retention: While linked + 6 months after deletion.

13. Technical & Security Data

What we collect: IP addresses (temporary, 30 days), device info, error logs (90 days), webhook statuses.

🔒 How We Protect Your Data

Security Measures:

🔐 Encryption: Bot tokens and sensitive data encrypted with Fernet
🗄️ Secure Database: Supabase, GDPR-compliant
🔑 Access Control: Only authorized staff can access personal data
🔍 Regular Audits: We review and update security practices
📉 Data Minimization: We only collect what's necessary

Data Storage: All user data is stored on servers located within the European Union, in compliance with GDPR requirements.

👥 Who Has Access to Your Data?

1. You (The User)

You can access your own data through /info command, Mini App dashboard, or support request.

2. Group Administrators

Admins of groups where you're a member can view your role, moderation history, trust score, and activity in their group only. They cannot see data about other groups.

3. Nexus Staff

Authorized staff may access data to provide support, investigate abuse, improve services, or comply with legal obligations.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Supabase (database, EU), Render (hosting, EU), Redis (caching), and Telegram (API). We do not sell your data to third parties.

⚖️ Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your data based on:

📋 Legitimate Interest: Providing public service for group protection, preventing abuse, enabling effective management

📝 Contractual Necessity: When you request services or purchase premium features

⚖️ Legal Obligation: When required by law

We do not require explicit consent for core bot functionality as these services are provided in the legitimate interest of public group management.

🛡️ Your Rights (GDPR)

✅ Right to Access

Request a copy of all personal data we hold. Free once every 30 days. Response within 30 days.

✏️ Right to Rectification

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data. Response within 30 days.

🗑️ Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data. Some data cannot be deleted (essential for public service). Deleting purchase history revokes access to purchased features.

🔒 Right to Restrict Processing

Request we limit how we use your data in certain situations.

📦 Right to Data Portability

Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

❌ Right to Object

Object to processing based on legitimate interest with your reasoning.

📢 Right to Lodge a Complaint

Complain to a supervisory authority if you believe our processing is unlawful.

👶 Children's Privacy

Nexus is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided us with data, contact us immediately.

🌍 International Data Transfers

Your data is stored and processed exclusively within the European Union. We do not transfer data outside the EU unless required by law, necessary to protect rights, or with your explicit consent.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We'll notify you by posting a notice in our bots, sending a Telegram message, or updating the "Last Updated" date.
