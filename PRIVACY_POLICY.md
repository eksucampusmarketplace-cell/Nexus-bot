# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 15, 2025

**Effective Date:** January 15, 2025

## 📋 Overview

Thank you for using Nexus, a Telegram group management bot and Mini App (collectively, the "Service"). At Nexus, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data.

By using Nexus (our Telegram bots @NexusBot and any cloned bots, as well as our Mini App), you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 🎯 Information We Collect

### 1. Telegram Account Information

**What we collect:**
- Telegram User ID
- First name, last name, and username (if set)
- Language preference
- Profile bio (if applicable)
- Account status information

**How we collect it:**
This information is automatically provided by Telegram's API when you:
- Start a private chat with any Nexus bot
- Join a Telegram group that uses Nexus
- Send messages in a group with Nexus
- Interact with our Mini App
- Are mentioned in a bot command

**Purpose:**
- Identify and authenticate users
- Enable moderation commands (e.g., `/ban`, `/mute`, `/info`)
- Provide personalized responses in your preferred language
- Maintain group membership records

**Retention Period:**
As long as your Telegram account exists, and up to 5 years after account deletion.

---

### 2. Group Membership & Activity Data

**What we collect:**
- Groups where you are a member
- Your role in each group (member, admin, owner, restricted, banned, left)
- Number of messages you've sent in each group
- Date of last message in each group
- Date you joined each group

**How we collect it:**
Automatically received from Telegram API when you interact with groups using Nexus.

**Purpose:**
- Enable group management commands (`/list`, `/inactives`, `/info`)
- Track activity for trust score calculations
- Help administrators manage their communities effectively

**Retention Period:**
As long as your Telegram account exists, and up to 5 years after account deletion.

---

### 3. Administrative Permissions & Roles

**What we collect:**
- Groups where you are an owner or administrator
- Specific permissions granted in each group
- Custom administrator titles (if set)
- Bot-side custom roles (moderator, chat cleaner, muter, helper, etc.)

**How we collect it:**
Through Telegram API when you are promoted as admin/owner, or when group managers assign roles via Nexus commands.

**Purpose:**
- Grant appropriate command access based on your role
- Allow you to manage settings via private chat or Mini App
- Display your role in `/staff` command

**Retention Period:**
As long as you maintain the role in the group, or until the group record is updated (typically within 2-7 days for active groups).

**Note:** This data is essential for the public service and cannot be deleted. To remove this data, leave the groups where you are an administrator.

---

### 4. Moderation Records

**What we collect:**
- Warnings issued to you in groups
- Timestamp of each warning
- Expiration date of warnings
- Reason for warnings (if provided)

**How we collect it:**
When group administrators use Nexus moderation commands to warn users.

**Purpose:**
- Maintain the warning system functionality
- Enable warning expiration and automatic actions
- Provide audit trails for group administrators

**Retention Period:**
Until warnings are removed by administrators, they expire, or your Telegram account is deleted (up to 5 years after).

**Note:** This data is essential for the public service and cannot be deleted.

---

### 5. Bot Clone Information

**What we collect:**
- Nexus bot clones you've created
- Clone usernames and bot tokens (encrypted)
- Clones you've started or blocked

**How we collect it:**
When you create bot clones via `/clone` command or the Mini App, or when you interact with cloned bots.

**Purpose:**
- Enable the bot cloning system functionality
- Allow you to manage your cloned bots
- Ensure clones work correctly across all features

**Retention Period:**
As long as your Telegram account exists (up to 5 years after deletion), and up to 1 month after a clone bot is deleted from Telegram.

**Note:** Deleting this data will cause your bot clones to stop functioning as Nexus bots.

When you interact with the Mini App or use commands that set preferences.

As long as your Telegram account exists (up to 5 years after deletion).

### 7. Captcha & Anti-Bot Data

**What we collect:**
- Groups you've joined but haven't completed captcha
- Captcha expiration time
- Permissions at time of joining
- Failed captcha attempts

**How we collect it:**
Automatically when you join a group with captcha enabled.

**Purpose:**
- Verify you're not a bot
- Allow captcha completion within the time limit
- Apply automated actions for failed captchas

**Retention Period:**
- Active captcha records: 30 minutes
- Failed captcha history: 2 months (to prevent abuse by re-joining)

**Note:** This data is essential for the public service and cannot be deleted.

## 🔒 How We Protect Your Data

### Security Measures

- **Encryption:** Bot tokens and sensitive data are encrypted using Fernet encryption
- **Secure Database:** All data is stored in Supabase, a GDPR-compliant database provider
- **Access Control:** Only authorized staff can access personal data
- **Regular Audits:** We regularly review and update our security practices
- **Data Minimization:** We only collect data necessary for our services

### Data Storage

All user data is stored on servers located within the **European Union**, in compliance with GDPR requirements.

## ⚖️ Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data based on the following lawful bases:

1. **Legitimate Interest** (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR):
   - Providing a public service for Telegram group protection
   - Preventing spam, abuse, and attacks in groups
   - Enabling effective group management tools
   - Calculating trust scores for community safety

2. **Contractual Necessity** (Article 6(1)(b) GDPR):
   - When you explicitly request services (e.g., creating bot clones)
   - When you purchase premium features
   - When you use our Mini App

3. **Legal Obligation** (Article 6(1)(c) GDPR):
   - When required by law to comply with valid legal requests

We do not require your explicit consent for core bot functionality, as these services are provided in the legitimate interest of public group management and cannot function without this data.

## 🛡️ Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

### 1. Right to Access (Article 15)
You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
- **How:** Contact us via `/support` command or email
- **Cost:** Free once every 30 days (small fees may apply for additional requests)
- **Response Time:** Within 30 days

### 2. Right to Rectification (Article 16)
You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **How:** Contact our support team
- **Response Time:** Within 30 days

### 3. Right to Erasure (Article 17)
You can request deletion of your personal data, with exceptions:
- **Cannot be deleted:** Data essential for the public service (see "What We Collect" sections above)
- **Can be deleted:** Preferences, support messages, purchase history (revokes access to purchased features)
- **How:** Contact our support team
- **Cost:** Free once every 30 days

### 4. Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18)
You can request we limit how we use your data in certain situations.
- **How:** Contact our support team

### 5. Right to Data Portability (Article 20)
You can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
- **How:** Contact our support team

### 6. Right to Object (Article 21)
You can object to processing based on legitimate interest.
- **How:** Contact our support team with your reasoning

### 7. Right to Lodge a Complaint (Article 77)
You can complain to a supervisory authority if you believe our processing is unlawful.
- **Authority:** Your national data protection authority
- **For EU users:** [Garante per la Protezione dei Dati Personali](https://www.garanteprivacy.it/)
