📋 Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

When you use Nexus Bot, we collect the following information:

  • User ID and Profile: Your Telegram user ID, username, and display name for bot functionality
  • Group Information: Group IDs, names, and member counts where the bot is added
  • Message Metadata: Message counts, timestamps, and moderation actions
  • XP and Game Statistics: Your gaming progress and achievements
  • Trust Scores: Calculated scores based on your group behavior

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide group management and moderation features
  • Calculate trust scores for automated moderation
  • Generate analytics and insights for group admins
  • Track XP and achievements for the gaming system
  • Improve bot features and user experience

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely with the following protections:

  • Encrypted database connections
  • Access limited to authenticated group admins
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Data retention limits - inactive data is purged

🔒 Important: We never sell your personal data to third parties. We do not share your data with advertisers or external analytics services.

Your Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR and similar privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Export your data in a standard format
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data

Contact & Data Requests

To exercise your privacy rights or ask questions about this policy:

  • Contact the bot owner through Telegram
  • Use the /support command in the bot
  • Email: privacy@nexus-bot.com

We aim to respond to all data requests within 30 days.

Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be announced through the bot and the effective date will be updated.
